DESCRIPTION

Finding a need for a better platform to communicate and share internal resources with employees, I designed and built a internal employee website.

The website is accessible to all firm employees. This website is the only place for employees to seek resources and blog posts for all firm topics. It is used by every employee on a regular basis.

I designed wireframes, planned the site structure, and designed all images and illustrations for the site. I trained a staff to handle site maintenance twice weekly.

Tools Used: HTML5, CSS3, Illustrator
