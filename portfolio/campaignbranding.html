Internal Campaign Branding

DESCRIPTION

I've branded and designed many materials with festive themes to excite and motivate participants in marketing campaigns. These materials have allowed me to explore illustration and brand consistency across channels.

Willy Wonka: 11x17 poster created to promote career development. Each task for professional development is illustrated as a room in Willy Wonka's factory.

The Amazing Race: Participants will move forward to a new location after achieving particular objectives. Required print and web branding materials.

Around the World: Participants earned a stamp in the passport for hitting milestones. Required print and web branding materials.

Road Trip: Participants earned miles to move forward on their journey. Required web branding materials and Cinco de Mayo themed event invite.

Tools Used: Illustrator and Photoshop
